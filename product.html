About this product

The LNK Creatives Whipped Body Butter is crafted with a nutrient-rich blend of African Shea Butter, Mango Butter and Cocoa Butter to restore moisture and create a protective barrier that keeps your skin soft and supple throughout the day.

Unlike heavy creams that sit on the surface, our airy whipped texture absorbs quickly, leaving no greasy residue — just silky, nourished skin from the very first use. Ideal for dry, sensitive or eczema-prone skin.

  • Intensely moisturises and softens dry skin
  • Absorbs quickly with no greasy residue
  • Soothes and calms sensitive and irritated skin
  • Improves skin elasticity and texture over time
  • Free from parabens, sulphates and artificial dyes
  • Vegan-friendly and cruelty free

Full Ingredient List

We believe in full transparency. Every ingredient we use is carefully selected for its skin benefit and sourced as sustainably as possible.

  • Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea Butter) — deeply moisturises and softens
  • Mangifera Indica (Mango) Seed Butter — rich in vitamins A & C, improves elasticity
  • Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Butter — creates a protective moisture barrier
  • Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil — lightweight, absorbs fast
  • Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Oil — balances and soothes
  • Arrowroot Powder — creates the signature non-greasy finish
  • Natural Fragrance (Vanilla Coconut)
  • Vitamin E (Tocopherol) — antioxidant, extends shelf life naturally

* Always patch test before use. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

How to Use

1
SHOWER OR BATHE
Cleanse your skin with warm water to open pores and prepare for maximum absorption.
2
PAT DRY
Gently pat skin until slightly damp — applying to damp skin locks in extra moisture.
3
SCOOP & WARM
Take a small amount and warm between your palms until it melts into a silky oil.
4
MASSAGE IN
Apply using circular, upward strokes across your body, paying extra attention to elbows, knees and heels.
5
ENJOY YOUR GLOW
Use daily morning and evening for best results. A little goes a long way.
